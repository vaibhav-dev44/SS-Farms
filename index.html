About Our Agritourism Farm

Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Nashik, S S Farms offers a unique agritourism experience blending traditional farming with modern comfort. Our farm preserves the rich agricultural heritage of Maharashtra while providing luxurious stays.

Each accommodation is designed to reflect local culture with farm-fresh decor, private balconies, and stunning views of vineyards and fields. Enjoy authentic Maharashtrian cuisine, farm activities, and proximity to vineyards and historical sites.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or an adventurous getaway, S S Farms is the perfect destination for families, couples, and groups.
